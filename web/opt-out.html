Opt-Out & Data Deletion

We respect your right to control your data and how it is used.

1. Uninstallation

You can stop all data processing immediately by uninstalling SmartPaste AI via the Google Workspace Marketplace Manage Add-ons menu.

2. Data Deletion Request

To request the permanent deletion of your credit history or associated email from our records, please send an email to schatwiti@sanadidari.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within 7 business days.

3. Local Storage

SmartPaste AI may use your browser's local storage for session management. Clearing your browser data will remove these temporary files.
