Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

1. Overview

SmartPaste AI ("the Add-on") is designed to simplify your workflow by intelligent data transmutation within Google Sheets. This policy details our commitment to your privacy.

2. Data Collection & Processing

Your privacy is our priority. SmartPaste AI does not store your spreadsheet data on its own servers. The data you paste is sent via secure, encrypted channels to Google's Gemini API only for processing and is not retained thereafter.

3. Google Scopes Used

  • spreadsheets: Used to insert cleaned and transmuted data into your active sheet.
  • script.container.ui: Used to display the assistant's sidebar interface.
  • script.external_request: Used to communicate with the AI engine and managing payment credits.
  • userinfo.email: Used to identify your account and synchronize your credit balance across sessions.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your data stays within the Google ecosystem.
